Ferret

A group of ferrets is called a “business.”

  • Scientific Name: Mustela putorius furo
  • Average Lifespan: 6-10 years
  • Average Length: 15 inches
  • Favorite Past-time: Snatching shiny objects

Ferrets are a fun, playful bunch of animals. They are energetic, bond closely with their owners and other ferrets, and are easy to litter train. Ferrets are susceptible to many genetic diseases, and require a lot of care.
